About the Healer

Dr. Devipriya RajahSP, BPT, MIAP.

I am Dr. Devipriya RajahSP, a seeker, healer, and guide walking the path of inner transformation for more than two decades. My journey began as a Physiotherapy Doctor, serving patients with dedication and compassion for 28 years.

Alongside the physical dimension of healing, I felt a strong inner calling toward the deeper aspects of energy, karma, and soul wisdom. Over the last 25 years, I have explored multiple holistic and spiritual healing practices.

My work includes Reiki Healing, Acupressure and Magnetic Therapy, Varmam and Number Therapy, Astrology Readings and Vaasthu Consultation, Tarot Card Reading, Pendulum Dowsing, and intuitive spiritual healing.

Through these practices, I realized that healing is not limited to the body. It involves the mind, emotions, ancestral patterns, energy systems, and the journey of the soul. Every individual carries unseen stories and inherited imprints that need to be understood with compassion and awareness.

This understanding led me to focus on Systemic Family Constellation Therapy and Akashic Healing Therapy. Through these methods, I support individuals in uncovering generational blocks, releasing emotional wounds, reconnecting with ancestral blessings, healing karmic patterns, and realigning with their purpose.

Today, my role is to guide individuals as they reconnect with their inner strength, clarity, and spiritual awareness. I believe that healing is a sacred journey of returning to oneself and remembering one’s true nature.

My mission is to help people heal their stories, reclaim their power, and awaken to their inner light.

Family Constellation Therapy

Healing approach that helps us understand how our family and ancestors influence our present life.

In Indian culture, we believe that we are deeply connected to our lineage, and that the experiences, emotions, and unresolved struggles of our elders can quietly affect our relationships, health, emotions, and life choices.

More

This therapy works by bringing awareness to these hidden family patterns in a safe and respectful way. During a session, what has been unspoken or forgotten within the family is acknowledged with compassion. When this happens, a natural sense of balance and peace is restored, and many people feel lighter, calmer, and more connected to themselves.


Family Constellation Therapy can support those facing repeated relationship issues, emotional pain, anxiety, grief, fear, or situations that seem stuck despite many efforts. It does not involve reliving trauma or blaming anyone. Instead, it allows healing to happen through acceptance, understanding, and gentle insight.


This approach goes beyond thinking and talking alone. It works at a deeper emotional and energetic level, helping individuals feel grounded, supported, and aligned with their natural life path. As harmony is restored within the family system, the benefits are often felt not only by the individual but across generations.


Benefits: Gain clarity in relationships, resolve recurring patterns, and feel emotionally lighter.
